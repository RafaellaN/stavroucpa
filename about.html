About Us

Stavroucpa is an established audit firm, comprising by a group of talented accountants having the higher academic degrees, professional qualifications and working experience. We are specialized in the field of Audit, Assurance, Business Advisory services, VAT compliance and Corporate and Personal Tax matters. We are a fully licensed audit and accounting Service Provider firm regulated by the Institute of Certified Public Accountants of Cyprus (ICPAC).

Our mission is to constantly provide high quality services to clients, built around the Clients' unique needs and deliver exquisite services by highly skilled professionals.

Our philosophy’s core value is a whole life ethical behavior, basing our business relationships on trust, transparency, confidentiality and independence.

Our strategy towards our strategic vision is to view the globe through our clients’ eyes. Applying the moto of “working closely with the client rather for the client” we have developed a thorough understanding of the clients’ needs, their work priorities, culture and beliefs, visions and expectations.

Our members have massive years of experience in working with a wide range of clients in diversified industries, including local and international groups and owner managed enterprises.

Our Vision

Stavroucpa vision is to become one of the best audit and tax service provider firm of the island and even beyond. Managements vision is for the firm to be well established in the Cyprus market and be one of the most preferred service providers.

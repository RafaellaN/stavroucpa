STATUTORY AUDITS

According to Cyprus’ Companies Law, all entities incorporated in the Republic have an annual obligation to submit to the Registrar of Companies its audited financial statements. Our audit approach is tailored to each client’s circumstances such as

  • The industry field in which they operate,
  • Their size and functions,
  • Their legal status.

It draws upon our technical know-how and experience on International Standards of Auditing, International Financial Reporting Standards and legislation.

INTERNAL AUDITS

The role of internal audit is to provide independent assurance that an organization’s risk management, governance and internal control processes are operating effectively. It is designed to help an organization accomplish its strategic objectives by bringing a systematic, disciplined approach by evaluating and improving the effectiveness of risk management, control, and governance processes.
